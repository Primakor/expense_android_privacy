Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Last updated: July 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  1. Google Account Information
    • Email address (used for Gmail integration)
    • Basic profile information (name)
    • Gmail read-only access for expense detection
  2. Financial Information
    • Expense descriptions
    • Transaction amounts
    • Expense categories
    • Transaction dates
  3. API Keys
    • OpenAI API key (stored locally on device only)

Information Collected Automatically

  1. Device Information
    • Device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • App version
  2. Usage Information
    • Features used
    • Sync frequency
    • Error logs (stored locally)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide Core Functionality
    • Scan Gmail for expense-related emails
    • Categorize expenses using AI
    • Track and display expense history
    • Generate expense reports
  2. Improve Our Services
    • Fix bugs and improve performance
    • Develop new features

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All expense data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain servers or cloud storage for your expense data
  • Your OpenAI API key is stored securely in encrypted preferences

Gmail Access

  • We use read-only access to Gmail
  • We only scan for expense-related emails
  • Email content is processed locally and not transmitted to any servers
  • Gmail access tokens are managed by Google's secure OAuth system

Third-Party Services

  1. Google Gmail API
    • Used to read emails for expense detection
    • Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  2. OpenAI API
    • Used to categorize expenses using AI
    • Only transaction descriptions are sent to OpenAI
    • Governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not:

  • Sell, trade, or rent your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Store your data on external servers

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  1. Access and Portability
    • Export your expense data at any time through the app
  2. Deletion
    • Delete all app data through the Settings menu
    • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  3. Control Gmail Access
    • Revoke Gmail access at any time through Google Account settings
    • Disconnect Gmail integration through the app
  4. Manage API Keys
    • Update or remove your OpenAI API key at any time

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Permissions Used
